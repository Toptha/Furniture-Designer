Philosophy

"Instead of a long running and steady battle with nature to dominate her, we can walk in step with a tree to release its joy, to join with her to realize her potentialities, to enhance the environments of man"

— Ayaka Mori













The Tree


THE DESTINY OF A TREE

A tree is perhaps our most intimate contact with nature. Each tree springs from a tiny seedling, like ourselves, and grows through youth, maturity, and old age. We believe a tree should be treated with the greatest respect, to be understood through our work, and to be realized through our craft.

Wood


SOLID WOOD

Wood is a living material, with its own life. It breathes, swells, and contracts with the seasons. The grain speaks to us of the tree's life and growth. We work with the wood, never against it, to bring out its individual character and beauty.

The Woodworker


A THOUSAND SKILLS

Woodworking requires many skills for the thousand jobs that must be done. Our studio calls upon a craftsman's determination, and love to build as perfect an object as possible.
